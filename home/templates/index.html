Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any additional questions or need further assistance, our support team is here to help. We're committed to providing you with the resources and guidance you need to navigate your trading journey successfully.

1. Do I need to be a coding expert to use your tools?

No, our platform offers user-friendly tools and a strategy builder that requires no coding skills, making it accessible for all users.

2. Can I test my trading strategies before using them?

Yes, our backtesting suite allows you to evaluate your strategies using historical market data to assess their performance before trading live.

3. How does social trading work?

Social trading allows you to follow and replicate the trades of successful investors, enabling you to benefit from their expertise without needing extensive knowledge yourself.

4. What kind of risk management tools do you provide?

We offer a range of risk management features, including customizable risk parameters and position sizing tools to help you protect your investments.

5. Is there customer support available?

Yes, our dedicated support team is available to assist you with any questions or issues you may have while using our platform.
